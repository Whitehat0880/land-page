Fitness Benefits

Improved Cardiovascular Health:
> Regular exercise can strengthen the heart, reduce the risk of heart disease, and lower blood pressure

Weight Management:
> Physical activity helps in weight control by burning calories and building lean muscle

Enhanced Muscle Strength and Endurance:
> Exercise can increase muscle mass and strength, making everyday tasks easier.

Flexibility :
>Stretching exercises and yoga can improve flexibility and reduce the risk of injury

Better Bone Health:
>Weight-bearing exercises, like walking or weightlifting, can help increase bone density and reduce the risk of osteoporosis

Boosted Immune System:
>Regular moderate exercise can enhance the immune system's ability to fight off infections
